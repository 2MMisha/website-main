About Us

2M Media is a digital agency specializing in web development, design, and competition management platforms. Founded in 2018, we've been helping businesses and organizations establish their digital presence with innovative solutions.

Our team consists of experienced developers, designers, and project managers who are passionate about creating high-quality digital products. We believe in combining functionality with elegant design to deliver exceptional user experiences.

From small business websites to complex web applications, we approach each project with the same level of dedication and attention to detail. Our flagship product, Rising Star Manager, has been successfully used to manage numerous competitions and events worldwide.

Rising Star Manager
Rising Star Manager is a comprehensive competition management platform that simplifies the organization of sports and artistic events. The system provides complete control over the entire competition process - from online registration of participants to automatic calculation of results and generation of certificates.

Key features:
  • Online registration with customizable forms
  • Automatic scheduling by categories
  • Digital judging system with real-time scoring
  • Dynamic results calculation and leaderboards
  • Certificate generation with custom designs
  • Participant management dashboard
  • Multi-language support
